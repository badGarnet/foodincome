Distribution of restaurants in New York City, NY

NYC is different from Houston, TX. Its local attractions, like Broadway, greatly skews the distribution of restaurants. Moreover, lots of New Yorkers don't drive/take a car to travel across town. Instead, they use subway. Therefore, the method of weighting income and household number by driving distance may not work so well in New York.

In fact, the logarithmic values for local income and restaurant count have a correlation coefficient (scroll down for it)! It could suggest restaurants in NYC is mainly driven by local customers.
