distribution of restaurants and income in Seattle, WA

The maps below shows how local income does not necessarily determine the number of restaurants in an area. For example zip code 98038 (south east of city) is one of the highest income areas (yellow color on income map), but it does not have a high restaurant density.

The accessible income evaluates the potential contribution from all over the city, instead of just the local zip code alone. As a result, places within easy reach to medium and high income areas in the city has high accessible income. Those areas also seem to have more restaurants.

The accessible income map shows there could be untapped potential in the east of the city where there is high accessible income but not that many restaurants.
